Sepal [ beta ]

Sepal is a plant collection manager for botanic gardens, arboreta, nurseries or private collections.

We're making Sepal available during the pre-release stage. During this time Sepal is free to use but we can't guarantee the safety of your account or your account data.

Plant collection management
Create and manage taxa, accessions and locations of plants in your collection.
Media management
Upload photos, videos, docs, etc and associate them with accessions and taxa.
User management
Create organizations and invite people to manage your collection and grant them different roles.
Open source
Open source so that your collection data isn't locked in to a proprietary system.
Cloud hosted
Sepal is hosted in the cloud so you don't need to worry about servers, backups, databases, etc.
